THE CIVIL PROJECT

THE PROJECT THAT LAYED THE FOUNDATION

The Civil Card project is a high-fidelity digital terminal designed to bridge the gap between cinematic storytelling and modern e-commerce. Built with a focus on "pixel-perfect" UI/UX, the platform serves as an immersive archive for premium Marvel collectibles, themed around the iconic conflict of the Sokovia Accords. By utilizing a high-contrast, "Classified" aesthetic and a custom rank-based asset system (SSS to S), the project transforms a standard collectionsping experience into a tactical mission where collectors can browse hero artifacts, manage their personal inventory, and track secure logistics through a tech-heavy, professional interface.
