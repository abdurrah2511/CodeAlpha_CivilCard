THE CONFLICT

The Civil Card archive was born from a world divided. Following the implementation of the Sokovia Accords, the hero community split into two distinct ideologies. On one side stands Team Stark, advocating for transparency and government oversight to ensure safety in a world of super-humans. On the other, Team Captain, fighting for the freedom to act without political interference. This project captures that tension, preserving the artifacts of a war that changed the Avengers forever. Every card in this collection is a record of a choice made in the heat of battle.

THE CREATOR

We don't just sell cards; we archive history. Our 'Civil Cards' are engineered with a high-grade aesthetic that reflects the tech-heavy world of Stark Industries and the rugged resilience of the Resistance. Each asset is categorized by a strict Rank System—from standard S-Rank field units to the ultra-rare Mythic artifacts. Featuring 'Pixel-Perfect' design, holographic finishes, and deep-lore metadata, these cards bridge the gap between digital fandom and high-end physical ownership.
